Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 19, 2024

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how ClipBot handles your information.

2. Information We Collect

  • OAuth Tokens: For authenticating with social media platforms
  • Video Metadata: Information about videos you upload
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the application

3. How We Use Information

We use collected information to authenticate with third-party platforms and upload videos on your behalf.

4. Data Storage

  • All credentials are stored locally on your device
  • We do not store your data on external servers
  • OAuth tokens are saved in local configuration files

5. Third-Party Services

ClipBot integrates with YouTube, TikTok, Pexels, and Pixabay APIs.

6. Data Security

We implement security measures including local storage only and no transmission to external servers.

7. Your Rights

  • Access your data
  • Delete your data
  • Revoke API access at any time

8. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions, please contact through GitHub Issues.
